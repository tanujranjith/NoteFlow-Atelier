Our philosophy: your data, your choice.

NoteFlow Atelier is built on a simple rule: productivity should not require giving up control. Your notes, tasks, and plans belong to you.

Privacy First
P

Local-first by default

Your workspace lives in your browser storage first. You decide when to export, import, or sync.

C

Consent-driven backup

Cloud backup is optional and uses your own Google Drive credentials, so the destination stays under your account.

T

Transparent by design

The codebase is fully auditable. Anyone can inspect how data is handled, stored, and exported.

Designed for deep work, not feature clutter.

Atelier keeps complex workflows practical: capture notes, schedule blocks, commit tasks, and monitor streak performance from one place.

Core Surface
N

Notes + Editor

Nested pages, slash commands, rich media embeds, tables, checklists, and clean formatting controls with live word count.

T

Tasks + Streaks

Commit daily focus tasks, track completion history, and keep long-running momentum through streak analytics and weekly freeze logic.

L

Timeline Blocks

Map your day visually with recurring or one-off blocks, then follow live progress and countdown in the current block panel.

S

Search + Structure

Use global and sidebar search, page tags, and hierarchical naming to navigate dense workspaces quickly.

A

Appearance Controls

Light and dark presets, custom colors, per-page application modes, typography controls, and reduced motion support.

D

Data Ownership

Local-first IndexedDB storage, JSON export/import, and optional backup to your own Google Drive credentials.

A daily loop that stays simple.

Use this rhythm to keep planning and execution connected without leaving the workspace.

Daily Flow
1

Commit your priorities in Today.

Select the tasks that matter now and keep your focus list intentional.

2

Block real time in Timeline.

Create time blocks for deep work, breaks, and admin so your calendar reflects reality.

3

Capture outcomes in Notes.

Use links, callouts, and media embeds to turn activity into reusable knowledge.

4

Review progress and streaks.

Weekly and monthly visuals show what is working and where your system needs adjustment.

Atelier includes:

  • 4 focused views: Today, Timeline, Notes, Settings
  • Task scheduling: one-off, daily, weekly custom days
  • Timeline recurrence: one-time, daily, weekdays, weekly
  • Built-in Flow assistant using your own Groq API key
  • Manual save, autosave, export/import, and Drive backup

Pick your interface style.

Both versions are local-first. Choose the one that matches how you like to work.

Atelier vs Classic

NoteFlow Atelier

Recommended for most users
  • Unified day workflow with timeline planning
  • Editorial visual system and modern controls
  • Expanded progress and streak surfaces
  • Theme customization and richer interaction model

NoteFlow Classic

Legacy interface
  • Familiar original workspace and interaction style
  • Good for continuity with older routines
  • Lighter visual shell and fewer ambient effects
  • Useful when you want a minimal transition path
