From the maker

Why Atelier exists.

I built Atelier because the tools I used never fit the full student workflow. Notes lived in one place, assignments in another, calendars somewhere else, and most productivity apps felt either too bloated or too generic. Atelier is the workspace I wanted: calm, local-first, private, and built around how students actually work.

— Tanuj Ranjith

Refined for depth.

A unified surface where planning and writing happen in the same state of flow. No cluttered dashboards, no distractions. Just your thoughts and your momentum.

N

Notes + Rich Editor

A beautiful surface for thought. Nested pages, slash commands, embeds, tables, and clean typing constraints. Complete with live word counts and elegant typography.

T

Tasks + Streaks

Commit priorities and track completion history. Keep your momentum visible with built-in streak analytics.

L

Timeline Blocks

Map real work sessions, recurring routines, and current-block progress from the exact same interface.

P

Privacy by Design

Local-first IndexedDB storage means your vault lives strictly on your machine. Complete data ownership with optional consent-driven cloud backups.
