Work should feel unhurried, private, and fully yours.

NoteFlow keeps the workspace online for seamless access. Local download and offline use remain an option, not the center of gravity. Everything is designed to support focus, not interrupt it.

Focus without friction

Minimal structure, maximum clarity. You can enter, work, and leave without the system asking for attention.

Control is optional
